How AgroTrace Works

We simplify supply chain transparency with a secure, three-step process powered by Blockchain technology.

1. Register & Mint

Farmers register produce lots and mint a unique digital token, creating a tamper-proof and traceable starting point.

2. Track & Transfer

Each step from farmer to distributor and retailer is recorded as a secure, immutable transaction on the Blockchain.

3. Verify & Trust

Consumers scan a QR code to view the complete history of their food, ensuring authenticity and building trust.
